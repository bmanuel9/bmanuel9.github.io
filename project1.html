Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients


1 1/2 cups Flour

2-3 Medium Bananas

1 cup of Sugar

1/2 tsp Baking Soda

1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Pinch of Salt

1/3 cup of butter (salted or unsalted)

1 Large Egg

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and butter an 8x4-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Stir in the melted butter afterwards.
  3. Mix in the baking soda, salt, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract. Then mix in the flour.
  4. Pour your batter into your prepared loaf pan. Bake for 55 to 65 minutes at 350°, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from oven and let it cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then remove from the pan and let it cool completely before serving.

SIDENOTE:

  • Sprinkling some flour in your loaf pan after buttering/oiling will help the finished loaf come out of the pan easier.
  • If you like your banana bread less sweet use 3/4 or 1/2 cup of sugar instead of 1 cup.
  • You can also feel free to add raisins if that's something you like in your bread.
